Frequently Asked Questions

If you want to start a new group (a new "channel"), simply click on "Create Channel" and give your channel a name. To invite people to your channel, go to the channel settings page (by clicking the cog-shaped icon) and either give your friends the channel code (which they will enter in by clicking Join Channel), or give them the sharing link by clicking the clipboard next to Share Channel.

You can join a channel by obtaining the channel code, clicking "Join Channel", and entering the code there. Users who are already in a certain channel can find that channel's code by clicking the cog-shaped settings button in the upper right corner of any channel page. Alternatively, you can join a channel by obtaining a sharing link, which is also found in the channel settings page.

To quote someone (to "verbatim" someone) in a certain group, simply click on a channel, type in what they said in the textbox at the bottom of the screen, click on the speaker button to select their name, and then click the submit button.

To guess who said what, click on the guess button next to any verbatim in a channel. This will bring up a dropdown, which you can click to see the available options. Select who you think said the verbatim. This will update the %correct on the right side of the page, which indicates the number of people who guessed correctly divided by the total number of guesses submitted for that verbatim.
(Note: if you posted the verbatim, you will not be able to guess.)

The leaderboard displays the top 3 best guessers based on the number of correct guesses they have in a certain channel. It is quite the achievement to be in the top 3!

Click on your profile picture icon in the upper right corner to access the user settings page. Once you make your changes, press the update button. You can return to the home page by clicking the Verbatims icon in the navbar.
